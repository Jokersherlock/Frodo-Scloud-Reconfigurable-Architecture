考虑矩阵的每个元素是是kbit的
$$
b_{00} = a_{00}*s_{00}+a_{01}*s_{10}+a_{02}*s_{20}+a_{03}*s_{30}=\sum^3_{j=0}a_{0j}*s_{j0}\\
s_{i0} = \sum^{k-1}_{n=0}s_{i0n}*2^{n}\\
b_{00} = \sum^3_{j=0}\sum^{k-1}_{n=0}a_{0j}*s_{j0n}*2^n\\
= \sum^{k-1}_{n=0}\sum^{3}_{j=0}a_{0j}*s_{j0n}*2^n\\
=\sum^{k-1}_{n=0}(a_{00}*s_{00n}+a_{01}*s_{10n}+a_{02}*s_{20n}+a_{03}*s_{30n})*2^n
$$
